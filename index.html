Brainstormer 2013

Welcome




Kolkata and Bengal continue to be a hot and happening place for quizzing activities in the country as there is no denying the fact that quizzing activities mainly started in Kolkata.

BRAINSTORMER is the annual inter-college/school quiz competition orchestrated by Phoenix. Since its inception in 2007, Brainstormer has seen a surge in the number of enthusiastic participants every year, with students from schools and colleges across West Bengal participating. Last year Mr. Barry 'o Brien, revered as a flamboyant quizmaster with international stature, was the quizmaster of the event and 93 teams participated in the event.


This year, we have decided to aloft Brainstormer to a completely new level by inviting students of schools run by NGOs to participate, with the hope that this will provide the students from marginal backgrounds to vie with the mainstream. In addition to that we are proud to announce our association with CRY(Child Rights and You).

This year Brainstormer will be held on 22nd September, 2013, at Khemka Hall (auditorium of Ballygunge Shiksha Sadan), Gariahat. Mr. Barry 'o Brien will be conducting the quiz. It is open for undergraduate and classes IX - XII students. Each team can consist of a maximum of three members. Participants in a team can be from different alma mater.


Juggle with mind-boggling questions and it may augur well for one’s contention to exciting prizes.

Thanking You,
Phoenix.

Rules






Though we don't want to impose anything, these rules are stipulated for a smooth functioning of the event....

It would be nice of you if you just follow the below mentioned rules and regulations so that we may conduct the event properly.

The rules are subject to change, by the PHOENIX committee in its sole and absolute discretion.

Rules and Regulations :

  1. >> Students of class IX to XII of schools of any Board whatsoever and Undergraduate students,persuing their Bachelor degree ONLY are allowed to participate.

  2. >> Passouts of Bachelor degree,that is students who have already completed their graduation is strictly NOT allowed to participate.

  3. >>Please make sure you bring your Identity Card (School ID/College ID/Library Card) on the day of the event.

  4. >>On-Spot registration will also be there,but its a subject to crowd and team space availability.So Teams are advised to register prior to the event in the website.

  5. >>Teams can comprise of students from different institutions(school/college).

About Phoenix






Phoenix is the official tech-club of Netaji Subhash Engineering College. It was established in 2006 under the able leadership of a few seniors of the college (seven to be precise). Starting from just seven, in seven years, Phoenix has grown to be the tech - hub of NSEC with a membership of over 200 students from various streams, and a full hierarchy of management.

Students indulged in ‘Phoenix-ing’ tend to develop various skills which boost their personality and shape up their professional career. They develop a sense of camaraderie, team spirit while working together in organising various events.

Together with Brainstormer, Phoenix also conducts Avenir, the inter- college tech fest of NSEC.
